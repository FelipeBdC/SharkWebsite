Sharks and Humans

What's this?

Sharks are very curious and intelligent. They investigate anything that interests them, sometimes causing accidental attacks on humans.

Cage-Diving

The use of bait can teach sharks to associate humans with food, altering their behavior and damaging the ecosystem

Finning

A practice that involves cutting of the shark's fins and throwing the still living, finless shark back in the ocean.
